Variance Argument (if needed)

If the garage placement requires a ZBA variance, the five-factor test is favorable:

1.
Reasonable Return: Only home on Squires Path without covered vehicle storage. Diminishes utility and resale.
2.
Unique Hardship: Nature Preserve on two sides eliminates ~50% of buildable envelope. Not shared by neighbors.
3.
Neighborhood Character: Under 600 sf, matching architecture. Multiple neighbors have garages. Screened by landscaping.
4.
Not Self-Created: Preserve boundaries set in 1968 subdivision. Owners purchased 2021 with pre-existing constraints.
5.
Minimum Variance: Smallest feasible garage. Property stays at ~14% building coverage vs 20% max.

ZBA: 159 Pantigo Road, (631) 324-4143. Typical timeline: 2–4 months.

Engage Zoning Attorney

2

Engage Architect for Site Plan

✓

3

Solicit 3 Contractor Bids

Get bids from J.A. Land Development, All Purpose Paving, APM O'Neill. Can run concurrent with permitting. Week 3–6.

4
